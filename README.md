# Piki

Piki is a local-first LLM Wiki for personal knowledge management.

It helps you turn raw notes, links, files, and fragments into a structured knowledge base that stays readable, portable, and fully owned by you. Your content lives as local Markdown files, so you are never locked into a proprietary format.

## Why Piki

- Local-first: your knowledge stays on your machine.
- Plain Markdown: easy to inspect, edit, and move.
- AI-maintained structure: keep a wiki organized without manually curating every page.
- No vendor lock-in: your files remain usable in editors like Obsidian, VS Code, or Vim.

## Installation

1. Open [Releases](https://github.com/NeilChenyc/piki/releases/latest).
2. Download the latest macOS build.
3. Move Piki to your Applications folder and launch it.
